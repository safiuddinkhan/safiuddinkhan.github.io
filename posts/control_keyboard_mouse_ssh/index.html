How to control the keyboard and mouse of your compute remotely via SSH

2022-03-23 09:08:47 +0500 PKT Technology Linux Raspberry Pi

To control the keyboard and mouse of your computer remotely via SSH follow the following steps.

  1. SSH into your system with X tunning enabled by putting "-X" flag at the end of your SSH command “ssh <user>@<ip_address> -X”

  2. Install x2x package on your remote computer. If you are running Debian/Ubuntu based distro then type “sudo apt install x2x” on your remote computer to install the package.

  3. In your SSH session type “x2x -to :0.0 -west” if your remote computer is on the left side of your monitor or “x2x -to :0.0 -east” if it is on the right side of your monitor and vice versa.

  4. Move your mouse left or right of your monitor depending on the "-west" or "-east" flag you have given as if your remote computer is an extra monitor attached to your system and take over that computer.
